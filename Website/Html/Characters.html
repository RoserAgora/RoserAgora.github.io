Genshin Impact's Characters

Genshin Impact has 42 unique characters and each having one of the seven (currently 6 developed) elements:

  • Pyro
  • Geo
  • Electro
  • Anemo
  • Hydro
  • Cryo
  • Dendro (unreleased)

Each character has one of five weapon classes:

  1. Claymore
  2. Pole-arm
  3. Catalyst
  4. Bow
  5. Sword

Genshin Impact releases updates around once every one or two months, right now the upcoming update is the 2.3 update that will include two new characters
